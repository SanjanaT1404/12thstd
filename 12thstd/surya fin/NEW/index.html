Lesson Plan

SDG Title Subject Topic Activity
SDG 1: No Poverty Social Studies Global Poverty Show a documentary video discussing global poverty.
SDG 1: No Poverty History Economic History Conduct a quiz on historical events related to poverty and social reforms.
SDG 2: Zero Hunger Geography Food Distribution Analyze food distribution patterns using maps and create a video presentation on food deserts in urban areas.
SDG 2: Zero Hunger Biology Sustainable Agriculture Design an infographic illustrating sustainable agricultural practices and their benefits.
SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being Health Science Mental Health Games that focus on the importance of mental health and coping strategies for students.
SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being Nutrition Physical Activity Create a quiz on the benefits of physical activity and healthy habits for students.
SDG 4: Quality Education Psychology Learning Theories Show a video explaining various learning theories and their impact on education.
SDG 4: Quality Education Computer Science E-Learning Technologies Create a tutorial guide on effective e-learning platforms and their features.
SDG 5: Gender Equality Sociology Gender Roles Produce a video that interviews individuals about their experiences with gender roles in society.
SDG 5: Gender Equality Literature Feminist Literature Organize a debate on key feminist texts and their authors, discussing their relevance today.
SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation Environmental Science Water Conservation Techniques Create a how-to video on simple water conservation techniques for households.
SDG 6: Clean Water and Sanitation Biology Aquatic Ecosystems Conduct a hands-on experiment analyzing water samples from local sources and present the findings.
SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy Physics Energy Efficiency Show a video demonstrating experiments that showcase energy efficiency in everyday appliances.
SDG 7: Affordable and Clean Energy Environmental Studies Alternative Energy Sources Create a poster or infographic illustrating different types of renewable energy and their benefits.
SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth Business Studies Career Development Data Hunt: Use real data to make informed decisions that affect economic growth.
SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth Social Studies Labor Rights Conduct a role-play activity simulating workplace scenarios to discuss labor rights and employee protections.
SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure Technology Smart Cities Games that explore the technologies that make cities smarter and more sustainable.
SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure Engineering Infrastructure Needs Develop a group project to design a sustainable infrastructure plan for your community.
SDG 10: Reduced Inequality Political Science Social Policies Produce a video explaining different social policies aimed at reducing inequality.
SDG 10: Reduced Inequality Economics Wealth Distribution Conduct a mock town hall meeting discussing solutions to address income inequality in your community.
SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Urban Planning Community Development Show a video showcasing examples of successful community development projects in different cities.
SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities Geography Urban Green Spaces Organize a field trip to a local park or green space to study its impact on urban biodiversity and community well-being.
SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production Environmental Science Conservation Access NASA’s Earth data and create groups to analyze the data to identify trends related to consumption and production
SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production Economics Consumer Awareness Organize a "sustainable shopping challenge" where students find and present sustainable products available in local stores.
SDG 13: Climate Action Environmental Science Climate Resilience Play Game
SDG 13: Climate Action Statistics Climate Data Analysis Conduct a hands-on data analysis project using local climate data from NASA databases to create visualizations and present findings.
SDG 14: Life Below Water Marine Biology Marine Ecosystems Produce a video exploring the diversity of marine life and the threats they face.
SDG 14: Life Below Water Environmental Science Plastic Pollution Organize a beach clean-up activity and create a report on the types and amounts of waste collected using data from NASA databases.
SDG 15: Life on Land Biology Ecosystem Services Show a video showcasing local ecosystems and their importance for biodiversity and human well-being.
SDG 15: Life on Land Environmental Studies Wildlife Protection Develop a campaign to promote local wildlife conservation efforts, including social media outreach.
SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions Civics Rule of Law Do a play of a case in the courtroom to understand the law procedure.
SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions History Human Rights Organize a debate on historical events related to human rights movements and their significance.
SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals Business Studies Global Partnerships Produce a video highlighting successful global partnerships that address sustainable development challenges.
SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals Economics International Trade Organize a mock trade negotiation session to simulate international cooperation on sustainability issues.
